  • Elaborated by: Rafa Cuellar.
  • Origin: Chihuahua, 2020.
  • Elaboration: The headband is made of folded fabric with long strands. The socioeconomic position of an individual is symbolized by the length of their koyera. A short one indicates a low status; a long one indicates a higher standing within the community.
