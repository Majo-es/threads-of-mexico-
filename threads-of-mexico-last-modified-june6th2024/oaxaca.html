Oaxaca

Oaxaca is home to 12 distinct indigenous communities spread throughout 570 municipalities, a region with a wealth of ethnic diversity. An estimated 300 traditional Oaxacan garments are woven by craftspeople from cotton, wool, and silk. The exquisite flower embroidery on Mixes fabrics has allowed them to transcend boundaries. Their designs are dominated by the colors black and red, and they typically feature images of suns and magueys. The Chinantec textiles have a riot of colors, with splotches of purple, pink, and red. Their symbolism has to do with the world's beginnings and the coexistence of life and death.
